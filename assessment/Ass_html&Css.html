Tech Event Registration Webpage

The webpage includes a structured navigation bar with sections such as Home, Events, Register, and Contact. A visually appealing hero section with a background image enhances the overall design. The registration section collects user details through a form, while the contact section provides essential communication information. The layout is clean, organized, and professional, ensuring a smooth browsing experience across different devices and screen sizes.
